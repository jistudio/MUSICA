headline - about

inspiring creativity

Intermusica is an international classical music agency.

We enable the world's most talented artists to realise their full potential.

We represent conductors, instrumentalists, singers, composers and dance companies, tour orchestras internationally and develop award-winning projects.

Our headquarters are in London and we have colleagues working in Berlin, Beijing and New York.

Our services include:

  • Artist Management
  • International Touring
  • Communications & Marketing
  • Innovation & New Audiences
